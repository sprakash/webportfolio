Projects

HalApp is for those with Parkinson disease to practice their speech using renowned poet Hal Sorowitz's poems. Hal was diagnosed with Parkinson's disease and has continued his journey as a poet. You can choose a poem by Hal, read it and gauge your speech using waveform and meter, you can record the poem and also save the video of you reading it. The project was selected for Virtual Beings Developer Grants. Currently phase one is desktop use only.
Check out HalApp | Read more about Parkinson's Poetry
